ALOK KUMAR JAISWAL

(FOUNDER)
A legacy of over 10 years and over 50 thousand customers served – Chandan Opticals’ humble legacy started with our first retail store back in 2000 at saharsa,Bihar.

Founded by visionary Mr. Alok kumar jaiswal, the company was awarded the ‘Most Admired Retailer’s Award” in 2018 and 2020, followed by winning the coveted title of India’s Most Trusted Brand in 2015. With over 10 stores all over India, our newly launched website and Home Service initiative, we offer quality eyewear from a variety of both international and home brands.
